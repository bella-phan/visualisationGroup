States and Territories that people arrive to


Victoria and New South Wale are top two destinations attracting people to arrive. The average numbers of immigration people for the period from 2010 to 2020 are 129,795 for Victoria, 129,141 for New South wale and 83,240. They are also top States have the developed economy. They have the best national education institutions and high career opportunities. Tasmania and Northern Territory are places that have the lowest arrival people. While Tasmania is smallest state and an island of southeastern coast of Australia, Northern Territory's economy are not as developmental as other States.
